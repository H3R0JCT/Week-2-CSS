Trail or All-Mountain Bikes

Trail bikes are the most versatile type of mountain bike, and are designed to be ridden on a variety of terrains. They are perfect for riders who want a bike that can handle both uphill and downhill riding. Bikes in this category are typically lightweight and have a moderate amount of suspension travel, making them ideal for all-day rides. They are a great choice for riders who want a bike that can handle a variety of terrain types, from smooth singletrack to technical descents.
